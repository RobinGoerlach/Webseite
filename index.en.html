Personal Motivation and Goals

The world of IT has fascinated me for decades – to me, it’s not just technical work, but a chance to tackle complex challenges and develop sustainable, useful solutions. I’m particularly drawn to the possibility of creating real value through my work, whether by building stable server environments or user-friendly applications.

As a developer and administrator, I see it as my role not only to meet technical requirements but also to foster collaboration and knowledge sharing in projects. This belief drives me daily and motivates me to continually improve my skills and share my knowledge with others.

Career Journey

My journey in IT began over three decades ago, and since then, I've experienced a vast array of projects and technologies. With a background in mathematics and software development, I started in an era when IT was still in its infancy, allowing me to witness firsthand the rapid evolution of technology.

Over the course of my career, I’ve specialized in scalable, robust software solutions and have always considered the use of open-source technologies indispensable in my work. I'm especially proud of my ability to work in heterogeneous system environments, consistently maintaining high standards for security and efficiency.

Beyond technical expertise, I've learned how essential agile methodologies and team-oriented work are to successfully complete complex projects. These experiences have taught me to stay adaptable and continuously learn – skills that are crucial in today’s IT landscape.

Values and Principles

To me, IT is more than just technology – it's a philosophy. For over three decades, I’ve witnessed how technologies influence and shape our lives. Over time, open source has become a core belief of mine. I believe that knowledge and tools should be accessible to all, fostering innovation and promoting a fair, transparent digital world.

As a Linux system administrator and full-stack developer, I dedicate myself to designing systems that are efficient, secure, and easy to maintain. The security and reliability of the systems I oversee are non-negotiable – they reflect my personal commitment to quality.

For me, sustainability and innovation go hand in hand: by choosing and implementing scalable, resource-efficient technologies, I contribute to a future-oriented and environmentally conscious IT landscape. In my projects, I constantly strive for a balance between technical elegance and practical usability, making the work experience as smooth as possible for clients and users alike.

Technological Interests and Specializations

My technological focus is on Linux administration and the development of stable, scalable applications. With extensive knowledge of languages like C#, Java, and C++, and experience with MySQL and Microsoft SQL, I understand the importance of creating efficient and maintainable software solutions.

Beyond development, I am actively involved in optimizing server environments and networks. From virtual infrastructures with Proxmox to modern backup solutions and OpenVPN expertise – I ensure that IT infrastructures are not only efficient but also secure.

My philosophy in technology development is rooted in clean, maintainable code and continuous learning. This is why I consistently pursue further education, currently focusing on TypeScript, Docker, and JavaScript. I recognize that software is never truly “finished,” and regular refactoring is essential to maintain efficient and well-structured solutions.
